About CineStream

CineStream is your ultimate companion for discovering where to watch your favorite movies and TV shows. Our platform combines the power of OMDb and Watchmode APIs to provide you with comprehensive streaming information.

Whether you're looking for the latest blockbuster or a classic film, CineStream helps you find exactly where to watch it across various streaming platforms.

Key Features:

  • Search across movies and TV shows
  • Filter by type and year
  • Discover popular titles
  • Save your favorites to watchlist
  • Find streaming availability
